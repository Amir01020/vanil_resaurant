About Us

Welcome to Bellagio Restaurant - a luxurious venue in Philadelphia for weddings, parties, and special events. We create unforgettable experiences in a unique style. Accommodating from 100 to 400 guests, we offer personalized consultations and host only one event at a time for an exclusive experience.

Elegant Space: Our venue features Royal NeoClassical design with state of the art lighting system that can transform our venue to a venue of your dreams. Our venue features 16 feet High Ceilings, 1260 sq feet reception hall, 6200 sq feet Grand Hall, 500 sq feet of bridal suite with powder room, custom cut 8 foot wide crystal chandeliers, custom cut beveled mirrors, Virtual Dome Ceiling and many other details that make Bellagio Restaurant the perfect venue for your event.

Most Advanced Technology: To make your event unforgettable our venue features Stage with Line Array RCF Sound System, 48 counts of the most advanced stage lighting system, 40 feet long 4K resolution Screen Behind our Stage, Two 10 feet long 4K resolution screens behind the Bride and Groom Throne.

Tailored Menus: We create a unique menu for your event that will exceed your expectations and leave you with unforgettable tastes. Live Chef Demos: Experience our chefs' expertise firsthand as they create culinary wonders right before your eyes. Professional Team: Our skilled organizers, waitstaff, and chefs ensure a seamless event.
